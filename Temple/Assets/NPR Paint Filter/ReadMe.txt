The scene "Paint/Demo" demonstrates all kinds of filter effect.
The scene "MangeSpeedLine/Demo" demonstrates manga speed line camera effect.
The scene "WashPainting/Demo" demonstrates wash painting art style effect.
Just open demo scene, click play button and play with these effects.

Although shaders are optimized, camera effect is relative heavily on mobile platform.
Please use it carefully.
